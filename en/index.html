About us

Founded in 1950 to serve Turin's industry. Today, an international reference.

Ferrari & Carena was founded in May 1950 by Giuseppe Ferrari and Antonio Carena to meet the needs of Turin's industries for large mechanical components: spare parts for machine tools, presses and equipment often hard to find on the market.

With the best technology on the market and highly qualified technicians, we guarantee short delivery times, top-quality products and competitive costs.
